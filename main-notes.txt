
### **Module 1 Summary — Client Intake & Problem Definition**

1. **Define Purpose**

   * Collect structured client info
   * Understand process and treatment problem
   * Produce the problem definition summary

2. **Define Inputs**

   * **Client Identification:** company, facility, contact
   * **Process Overview:** fluid type, treatment stage
   * **Flow Characteristics:** average & max flow, standardized units
   * **Contaminant Information:** type, current/target concentrations
   * **Treatment Goals:** oxidation, disinfection, etc.
   * **Existing Infrastructure:** power, space, ventilation, pumps
   * **Constraints:** budget, deadlines, regulatory, safety

3. **Design Data Structure**

   * Store all inputs in a structured project record
   * Reusable across future steps

4. **Design Screens / UI Flow**

   * Multiple screens: Client Info → Process → Flow → Contaminants → Goals → Infrastructure → Constraints → Summary → Report

5. **Input Validation**

   * Ensure all entries are reasonable and complete
   * Prevent missing or incorrect data

6. **Unit Standardization**

   * Convert all inputs to standard units for calculations

7. **Preliminary Engineering Checks**

   * Basic feasibility: ozone suitability, estimated flow ranges
   * Highlight missing or problematic data

8. **Problem Definition Engine**

   * Summarize the process, problem, scale, and objectives
   * Identify gaps and risks

9. **Create Deliverables**

   * **Client Intake & Problem Definition Report**
   * Key sections: Client info, process description, contaminants, goals, infrastructure, observations, next steps

10. **Prepare Outputs for Next Step**

    * Standardized flow, contaminant list, treatment targets, site constraints
    * Checklist for Step 2: Site Assessment

11. **Progress Tracking**

    * Mark Step 1 complete → unlock Step 2

12. **Save Project**

    * Store all input data and generated summaries for future modules
